About O&M

At O&M, we believe skincare should be natural, effective, and sustainable. Our products are handcrafted with love using 100% plant-based and eco-friendly ingredients.

We started O&M in 2025 with a simple mission: create lotions and oils that nourish your skin while caring for the planet and its people. From lavender lotion to coconut oil, each product is carefully made in small batches for the best quality. Here at O&M, we’re proud to be a cruelty-free, eco-friendly, kid-friendly oil and lotion company in California. We are dedicated to bringing you skincare that makes you feel confident and radiant, and gives you that special feeling of being moisturized.
